Student Corps

Student Corps is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization that encourages participants to partake in volunteer activities. Student Corps offers the Presidential Volunteer Service Award, which is an award that awards volunteers for their hard work in helping the people. Student Corps encourages students to volunteer at any organization.

The mission of the Student Corps is to introduce students to the community role they will play in the future, through the following key ideas:

Opportunity Providing

To provide students opportunities to be leaders in the school, town, and wider communities.

Gain Perspective

To help students gain larger world perspectives through volunteer experiences.

Relation-Building

To promote communication and collaboration between students to create a network between the leaders of the future.
